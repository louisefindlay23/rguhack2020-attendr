About RGU Attend

RGU Attend is a student attendence app from Attendr. Simplifying register taking across universities.

RGU Attend consists of two apps, one for students and one for lecturers.

Through Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), the Student app connects to the Lecturer app to record your attendance in lectures and labs.

This makes signing into classes easier for students since they don't have to fumble around for a pen and saves time for lecturers who don't have spend time finding the sheet of paper.

The registration was quick and intuitive, I liked that it took live registration I could observe. I was also happy with the manual registration, helping me mark students in who either didn’t have the app or that where apprehensive about using Bluetooth in this manner.
