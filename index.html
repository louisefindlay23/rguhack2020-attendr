User Reviews

We've already received (through a student questionnaire) very favourable feedback suggesting the idea is viable (e.g., an average rating of 4.86 out of 5 for ease of use) and received several positive comments, such as:

Using the app is easier since I don't have to worry about not having a pen nor do i have to worry about whether I signed it or not.

It’s great to see new technologies entering the classroom. The same paper-based method has been in place for the 20 years I have been involved in teaching at RGU and has remained unchanged since I was a student. The paper-based approach is unreliable because students can sign in friends and for multiple classes at once; as a lecturer I haven’t an easy way to check accuracy and don’t really see it as my job to check. Attendr sorts these problems with an apparently simple solution, certainly simple to use in class.

The registration was quick and intuitive, I liked that it took live registration I could observe. I was also happy with the manual registration, helping me mark students in who either didn’t have the app or that where apprehensive about using Bluetooth in this manner. Overall - a positive experience with a few things to consider moving forward.
